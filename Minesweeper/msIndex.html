Minesweeper

Instructions:

  • The goal of the game is to uncover all squares without hitting a mine.
  • Click on a square to see what lies below, Right click a square to place a mine.
  • 3 difficulties of gameplay available: EAST(9x9 w/ 10 mines), MEDIUM(16x16 w/ 40 mines), and HARD(16x30 w/ 99 mines)
  • If a square doesn't contain a mine, it will display the number of mines touching that square.
  • If the number is 0, all nearby squares with a zero will be automatically cleared out.
  • Enjoy the game!!
